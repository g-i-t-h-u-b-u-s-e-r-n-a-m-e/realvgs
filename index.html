VeryGood School

One Account, Infinite Possibilities

Forget having your students keep track of dozens of different, highly similar usernames and passwords for all the services they use. With VeryGood School, your students only need to sign in to VeryGood School one time, after which they'll be able to log into any educational site in just a few clicks. This adds up to significant time savings for students; even if it only saves 15 seconds per login, it will save over an hour of total time after just 250 logins.

Security First

With VeryGood School, all of the student data for your school is stored locally with your school's IT department. We can't even access your student data! This means that even if we get hacked, all your student data would still be safe. The security also extends to the student experience. Students have 2-factor authentication with their school emails by default, which can be set to picture verification for younger students.

100% Free*

*No buts

With VeryGood School, our services are completely free for anyone to use. Not only do you not have to pay for it, the source code is also completely public and free to edit on our GitHub, so if you want to make any changes and know how to do that, you're welcome do do so! This not only helps school districts financially, it also helps ensure that everything is functioning the best it can.
